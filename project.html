Bubba: The Happy Buddy

An Android Wear app that encourages teenagers with eating disorders to maintain healthy eating habits through positive reinforcement and non-intrusive techniques.

The app has a virtual pet on the watch, named 'Bubba', who the user is encouraged to feed everytime the user eats. After feeding Bubba, the user is asked how he/she feels. Consistent eating habits are reflected in leveling up, earning achievements, and tracking your data. An 'SOS Button' pairs the user up with his/her guardian and notifies the guardian whenever the teenager falls below healthy eating habits.
